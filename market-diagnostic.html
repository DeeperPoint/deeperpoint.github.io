Market Engineer's Diagnostic

Walk through a structured assessment of your market's thin market challenges. Based on the framework in our whitepaper.

Before You Begin

This diagnostic walks you through the key structural, existential, and resistance factors that determine whether a market is thin, why it is thin, and where engineering interventions can make the greatest difference.

There are 6 sections with a total of about 30 questions. You can skip any question — incomplete answers still produce a useful profile. At the end you'll get a summary of your market's challenge profile with suggested engineering responses.

Your answers stay in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
